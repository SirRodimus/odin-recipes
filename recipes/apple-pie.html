Apple Pie

Home

Description

Apple pie is a classic dessert made with a flaky crust and a sweet, spiced apple filling. The apples are typically mixed with sugar, cinnamon, and sometimes nutmeg, creating a warm and comforting flavor. Baked until golden brown, the crust becomes crisp while the filling turns soft and juicy. Often served warm, apple pie is a timeless treat enjoyed on its own or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Pie crust
  • Lemon juice

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Peel, core, and slice the apples.
  3. Mix the apples with sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour, and lemon juice.
  4. Place one pie crust in a pie dish.
  5. Add the apple mixture into the crust.
  6. Dot the filling with small pieces of butter.
  7. Cover with the top crust and seal the edges.
  8. Cut small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  9. Bake for 45–50 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  10. Let the pie cool before serving.
