Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Known for its comforting and hearty flavors, it is often baked until golden and bubbling. Each layer blends together to create a satisfying combination of textures and tastes, making it a favorite for family meals and gatherings. Whether prepared traditionally or with creative variations, lasagna remains a timeless and beloved comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef in a pan and add tomato sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with an egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta mixture, then mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  8. Top with parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake for 30–40 minutes until hot and bubbly.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
