Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a popular baked treat known for their soft, chewy texture and rich, sweet flavor. Made with a buttery dough and filled with chocolate chips, they are baked until golden brown on the edges while remaining tender in the center. These cookies are a classic favorite, perfect for snacks, desserts, or sharing with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract and mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Let cookies cool before serving.
